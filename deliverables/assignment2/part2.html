# Part 2: Mid-fidelity Wireframes

# Introduction

For Part 2 of this project, you will be "upgrading" your Low-Fidelity Wireframes. By this I mean that you will be adding some styling like; drop shadows, corner radiuses, etc.

Think of Part 2 as a stepping stone between Low-fi and High-fi wireframes. Mid-fi wireframes will also help inform the type of components and styles you'll have to create in Part 3.

In addition to adding styles, you will also need add arrows between your screens to show the flow. Please refer to week 9's information about Flow Diagram Wireframes.

# Instructions

  • Make a copy of your "Part 1 - Low-fidelity Wireframes" in the pages, update for Part 2.
  • Within the new page you have created, start applying styles and effects to your wireframes.
  • At this point, do not add any colour.
  • You may also decide to add images, just make sure you remove any saturation.
  • Once you have completed adding your effects and styles, add arrows showing the flow of your screens(how your screens are connected).

Do not worry about making this deliverable look like a real website.
